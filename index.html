This map combines real-time traffic conditions from Google and traffic cameras provided by the New York State Thruway Authority, New York State Dept. of Transportation and Westchester County. The color of the traffic conditions is based on the following:
Green: more than 50 miles per hour or 80 kilometers per hour
Yellow: 25 - 50 miles per hour or 40 - 80 kilometers per hour
Red: less than 25 miles per hour or 40 kilometers per hour
Red/Black: very slow, stop-and-go traffic
Grey: no data currently available
